Borrowing and Returning Library Books

Library ID Card

  1. Your student ID card serves as your Library Identification Card. You must present your own ID card to borrow from the Library.
  2. You will not be allowed to borrow material without your card.

Borrowing

To borrow material present your Identity card to library staff at the Library Circulation Desk. Books may be borrowed for a period of two weeks.
Books returned late are subject to a fine of Tshs 500 per day. Repeated late return of books will result in a suspension of borrowing privileges.
You will not be allowed to take out books until all overdue books are returned and fines are paid.

Renewals

The loan period of a book may be extended twice, provided no other student has reserved the book.

Reserving a book

To reserve a book which is on loan to another reader, please ask a member of Library staff for assistance. By reserving a book you ensure that the book will be held for you upon its return.
Books will be held for one week and then returned to open circulation.
It is your responsibility to collect a reserved book.
